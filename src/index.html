The Pmodoro Timer

Short work cycles to keep you focused and productive

25:00

How it Works

  1. Decide on the task...
  2. Start the timer
  3. Work on the task for 25 minutes
  4. When the timer finishes, take a 5 minute break (i.e. check email, get a cup of water, etc.)
  5. Repeat steps 2 through 4 three times
  6. After the 4th cycle, take a longer break (15-30 minutes)
